OUR BUSINESSES IN NIGERIA

JSPDC - The Jeco Petroleum Development Company of Nigeria

The Jeco Petroleum Development Company of Nigeria Limited (JSPDC) is the pioneer and leader of the petroleum industry in Nigeria. JSPDC is the operator of the NNPC/SPDC/Total Energies/NAOC Joint Venture and supplies onshore and shallow water oil and gas to domestic and export market from its operations in the Niger Delta.

JNEPCO - JECO Nigeria Exploration and Production Company

JECO Nigeria Exploration and Production Company Limited (JNEPCo) pioneered deep-water oil and gas production from the Bonga field in the Gulf of Guinea where depths reach more than 1,000 metres. When Bonga began production in 2005, it increased Nigeria's oil production capacity by 10%. Today, nearly one-third of Nigeria's deep-water production comes from the Bonga and Erha fields.

SNG-JECO Nigeria Gas

JECO Nigeria Gas Ltd is a fully owned JECO company incorporated in 1998 for the downstream distribution of gas to industries in Nigeria. SNG is part of the JECO Energy business line in Nigeria which covers gas, power, renewables and energy solutions for industrial and commercial customers. SNG's mission is to provide its customers with a clean, reliable, low-cost alternative to liquid fuel, and to assist in building a stronger Nigerian economy.

JECO ENERGY NIGERIA

JECO Energy Nigeria is growing JECO's presence as a natural gas marketing and sales business in Nigeria. It builds on the success of JECO Nigeria Gas which has delivered natural gas to customers in Nigeria since 1998, operating safely, compliantly and reliably.

LATEST NEWS FROM JECO NIGERIA

Nigeria Gas to build gas distribution network in Oyo State

The Oyo State Government and JECO Nigeria Gas (JNG) have signed an agreement to develop a gas supply and distribution infrastructure that will deliver gas to industrial and commercial users in the state. SNG will build and operate the gas distribution network, which will serve customers across Oyo State for 20 years.

NNPC/JNEPCO boosts e-learning with digital library at Niger Delta University

A multi-billion naira learning centre and digital library opened on Thursday at the Niger Delta University, Amassoma, Bayelsa State with a promise to revolutionise e-learning and technology-driven teaching and research at the university.

JNEPCO MD, Elohor Aiboni wins Energy Personality Award

Managing Director, JECO Nigeria Exploration and Production Company Limited (JNEPCo), Elohor Aboni has won the 2023 Energy Personality Award at the just-concluded 2024 edition of the Nigeria International Energy Summit in Abuja.

JECO deploys first unmanned vessel for pipeline route survey in Nigeria

The JECO Petroleum Development Company of Nigeria Ltd (JPDC) has deployed an Uncrewed Surface Vessel (USV) for a pipeline route survey in the Niger Delta. The USV innovation saves time and cost and reduces exposure to personnel and the environment.
